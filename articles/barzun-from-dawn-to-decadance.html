Introduction

Barzun's Dawn to Decadance was his last book before he died; and it was a synthesis of his learning. It's a wonderful history book describing Western Civilization over the last 500 years; and contains a lot of recommendations of other books to read. I read the book a few years ago, and recorded all the books, and I am finally getting to put it down on the internet.
